Our Product

Pre Rent Guarantee

This product was created for people who have not found desired housing yet. Pre rent guarantee is a document that can be presented to a prospective landlord to showcase your financial reliability. To make you the landlord’s top choice and help in their apartment search.

Rent Guarantee

Rent Guarantee Payfi gives you access to housing as an immigrant by standing in as your rent guarantor to your landlord. This ensures that no huge deposit, credit score, rental history, co-signers  or additional guarantors are requested from you.

Credit Card Rent Payment

Payfi has introduced a Credit card rent payment gateway such that when you pay your Rent through us, we will report each on time rent payment to Equifax Canada.

Build your credit score with each rent payment you make through Payfi

Each on-time rent payment you make gets reported to Equifax Canada who in turn updates your credit score.

Features Payfi: Empowering Immigrants with cost-effective housing solutions to unlock their career and educational successes.


Picture this: You arrive in a new country, and boom! finding a place to stay is even more challenging than you expected. We get it because we've been there too. That is why Payfi was created - to be the support we needed when we were new here.

Payfi is here to make your life easier by eliminating the need for a credit history, guarantor, or paying multiple months' rent upfront. We're not just a platform; we're your partners in helping you secure housing that might have seemed impossible. 

Our goal is to protect landlords' rental income and assure them that newcomers like you are reliable tenants, without the need for a guarantor. Let's make finding a place to live stress-free together! 

Get Started

Get started in 4 easy Steps:


  • Sign up on app.payfi.ca
    • Create your account by filling out the required information such as your name, email address, password, and referral code (If you have one). Once you submit the form, confirm your email address by entering the OTP sent to your registered email.
  • Fill in your details and verify your mobile number
    • Fill out your personal information and verify your mobile number by entering the OTP sent to you.
  • Complete Liveness detection for added security
    • Follow the prompts displayed on your screen to verify your identity.
  • Upload all Necessary Documents
    • Verify your international passport & upload all necessary documents based on your status e.g., study permit, work permit, proof of funds, Permanent residency card, 4-month recent bank statement, etc.
